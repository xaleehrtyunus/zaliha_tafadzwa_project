Umar Musa Yar'adua

The Life of Umar Musa Yar'adua is as follows:

  • 1912 - Umar Musa Yar'adua is born
  • 1928 - He gets his education at Katsina College
  • 1930 - Musa begins his career in teaching at Katsina Middle School
  • 1999 - He serves as the Governor of Katsina state, and he is a member of the People's Democratic Party (PDP) up to 2007
  • 2007 - Umar Musa Yar'adua is voted the President of Nigeria
  • 2007 - 2010 He does the following amoung others:
    1. Reduction of Petrol pump price from 75 naira to 65 naira
    2. Declaration of all his assets setting a template for others to follow
    3. Approval of the Dredging of River Niger
    4. Granting of Amnesty to Niger-Delta Militants
    5. Construction of the Kaduna-Abuja railway
  • 2010 - On the 5th of May, Umar Musa Yar'adua dies
